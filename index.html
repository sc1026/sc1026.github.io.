About

Data analyst with experience using Excel/Tableau/SQL/Python to obtain insights from a wide variety of data sources. Excels at the full range of project life cycle in regards to: gathering requirements and business objectives, exploratory data analysis, model building, & presenting insights. Data has always intrigued me. I believe that data can tell fascinating stories. I’ve used data for many different tasks, such as helping to turn seemingly incomprehensible information into recommendations to help improve products, services, or experiences. I'm still on a mission to create bigger, better, and more useful things. Over the past few years, I've learned to code, help launched a website, analyzed data for research, and expanded my data analytics toolbox. My fiancée says that I like to keep busy; I say that there's a method to my madness. I am a data analyst who lives at the intersection of technology, art, and content creation. Outside of work, I spend my time developing my website, mentoring and tutoring students, and doing service work. Connect with me if you want to diversify the tech industry, and you're interested in building meaningful partnerships that produce measurable results.
